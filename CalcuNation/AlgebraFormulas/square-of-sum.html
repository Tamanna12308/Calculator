Step-by-Step Solution

(a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²

    Description of the Formula

    The square of the sum formula states that when you square the sum of two numbers, the result is the sum of the squares of the numbers plus twice their product.

    Formula History

    The Babylonians and Greeks had a strong understanding of algebra and geometry, which laid the foundation for understanding and using such formulas. This formula is fundamental in algebra and is widely used in expansions, equations, and simplifications.

    Practice Problems

    Problem 1: Find the expansion of (5 + 3)².

    (5 + 3)² = 5² + 2(5×3) + 3² = 25 + 30 + 9 = 64

    Problem 2: If a = 7 and b = 2, find (a + b)².

    (7 + 2)² = 7² + 2(7×2) + 2² = 49 + 28 + 4 = 81
